What You Get

  • Preliminary Roost Assessment (PRA): daytime inspection, photos, roost potential classification, and recommendations.
  • Bat emergence/return surveys: dusk or dawn survey effort, field species ID, deployment summary data, and analysis included.

Thermal & Acoustic Review

Ecologist-led review of thermal and video survey footage, integrated with acoustic analysis to support robust interpretation of bat behaviour and roost use.

Price

£100 per deployment

Request a Quote

What You Get

  • Timestamped thermal and video behaviour log (emergence, return, foraging, commuting)
  • Integrated acoustic analysis to inform species or species-group identification
  • Cross-referencing of thermal events and acoustic detections for confident interpretation
  • Annotated video clips and stills of key behavioural events
  • Clear written summary to support roost characterisation and reporting

Inputs needed: Thermal or video files, associated acoustic data (or confirmation of concurrent recording), survey date/time, camera and detector locations, and site context.

Typical turnaround: 1–3 working days per survey night (volume-dependent).
